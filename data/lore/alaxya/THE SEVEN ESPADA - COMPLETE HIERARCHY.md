## **1\. WHAT ARE THE ESPADA?**

### **Definition:**

The **Seven Espada** are Nivar'Theon's elite warriors and administrators \- the highest-ranking individuals in Alaxya below the Sovereign himself and the Silent Guardians. They serve as his personal weapons, enforcers, and representatives across the empire.

### **The Name:**

**"Espada"** (Spanish for "sword") \- They are the Sovereign's seven blades, each deadly in their own way.

### **Requirements to Become Espada:**

**Before The Horning:**

* Must possess **at least 3 Crest-rank runes**  
* Demonstrated exceptional combat ability  
* Proven loyalty (or usefulness) to Nivar'Theon  
* Selected personally by the Sovereign

**The Exception:**

* Pure-blood dragons (like Thelyssra) don't need runes  
* Their inherent power qualifies them

### **Authority:**

**The Espada Have:**

* Command over military forces  
* Authority over Noble Houses in their regions  
* Access to Deep Dungeons (restricted areas)  
* Direct communication with Nivar'Theon  
* Nearly unlimited resources  
* Right to judge and execute (with limitations)

**The Espada Cannot:**

* Defy Nivar'Theon directly  
* Kill other Espada without Sovereign permission  
* Act against Vritra Purebloods  
* Refuse direct orders from Silent Guardians

## **2\. THE HORNING \- BECOMING ESPADA**

### **The Ritual:**

**What Happens:**

* Nivar'Theon selects a candidate  
* In private ceremony, he **implants a fragment of his antler** into their skull  
* Antler piece grafts to bone, becomes part of them  
* **Most candidates die** during the process (rejection)  
* Survivors gain immense power boost

**Physical Changes:**

* **Horns grow** from the implant site (1-4+ horns)  
* Number of horns is **purely aesthetic** (not power-based)  
* Horn style varies by individual  
* Cannot be removed without killing the Espada  
* Scars may appear around implant site

**Power Gained:**

* Massive increase to physical abilities  
* Enhanced magical capacity  
* Direct connection to Nivar'Theon (he can sense betrayal)  
* Access to some of his stolen clan powers  
* Near-immortal lifespan (aging slows dramatically)

**The Cost:**

* Bound to Nivar'Theon's will through antler  
* He can cause pain through the connection  
* Betrayal triggers agonizing feedback  
* Death if antler removed  
* Loss of some free will

## **3\. THE HIERARCHY (RANKS 1-7)**

### **How Ranking Works:**

**NOT Pure Power:**

* Rank ≠ strongest fighter  
* Based on **combination** of:  
  * Combat ability  
  * Strategic value  
  * Loyalty/reliability  
  * Usefulness to Nivar'Theon's plans  
  * Political influence  
  * Specialized skills

**Rank Determines:**

* Authority over lower-ranked Espada  
* Access to restricted information  
* Priority for resources  
* Respect from Noble Houses  
* Command structure in military operations

**Can Ranks Change?**

* Rarely, but yes  
* Death creates vacancy (promoted up)  
* Nivar'Theon can reassign if needed  
* Inter-Espada duels sometimes settle disputes  
* **Current roster has been stable for decades**
